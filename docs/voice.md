# Voice & Audio Settings

Waifu Companion supports multiple text-to-speech engines and audio features.

## Text-to-Speech (TTS)

### Kokoro TTS (New)
The Kokoro TTS engine provides high-quality, natural-sounding voice synthesis.

**Features:**
- Multiple voice options
- Support for various languages
- Module-based loading for better performance

### WebSim TTS
The original built-in TTS provider.

**Configuration options:**
- Voice selection
- Language matching
- Stability and similarity sliders

### Enabling Voices
Each TTS engine can be independently enabled/disabled:
- Primary Voice (WebSim)
- Kokoro Voice

## Speech-to-Text (STT)

Use your microphone to speak to your companion:
1. Click the microphone icon in the chat input
2. Speak your message
3. Click again to stop recording
4. Message is automatically transcribed and sent

## Internet Radio

Stream music from Listen.moe:
1. Go to Settings > Audio Settings
2. Toggle the radio on/off
3. Volume can be adjusted in the same section

## Sound Effects

UI interactions produce subtle sound effects powered by Tone.js:
- Button clicks
- Panel opens/closes
- Chat message send/receive

## Audio Settings

- **Master Volume**: Overall audio volume
- **TTS Volume**: Text-to-speech volume
- **Radio Volume**: Internet radio volume
- **Sound Effects**: Toggle UI sound effects on/off
